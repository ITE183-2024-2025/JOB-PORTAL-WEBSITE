A Job Portal Website is a comprehensive online platform that connects job seekers with employers across various industries. This website allows candidates to create profiles, upload resumes, and search for job openings based on keywords, location, industry, or job type. Employers can post job listings, filter applicants, and manage recruitment processes efficiently. With features like advanced job search, job alerts, personalized recommendations, and company profiles, a job portal website aims to streamline the hiring process, making it easier for job seekers to find their ideal roles and for companies to identify top talent.
